## 📖 Overview

<a href="#coinLedger">CoinLedger</a> serves cryptocurrency investors, traders, DeFi participants, and NFT collectors who have taxable cryptocurrency activity and need accurate tax reporting without spending dozens of hours manually tracking and calculating every transaction.

The <a href="#coinLedger">active trader tax reporting use case</a> calculates gains and losses across hundreds or thousands of trades across multiple exchanges — the volume of transactions that active traders accumulate makes manual calculation practically impossible, and CoinLedger's automatic import and calculation makes it manageable. The <a href="#coinLedger">DeFi participant reporting use case</a> handles the complex transaction types — yield farming income, liquidity provision rewards, governance token distributions — that DeFi activity generates and that standard tools misclassify or miss entirely.

The <a href="#coinLedger">NFT collector reporting use case</a> tracks NFT purchases, sales, and the complex cost basis situations that NFT trading creates — including gas fees, marketplace fees, and the wash sale considerations that cryptocurrency taxation involves. <a href="#coinLedger">Tax professional collaboration use case</a> generates the complete transaction history and tax report that CPAs and tax professionals need to prepare cryptocurrency tax returns for their clients — providing organized, accurate data that reduces the time tax professionals spend reconstructing transaction history.</p>

<a href="#coinLedger">Year-round tax planning</a> uses CoinLedger throughout the year rather than only at tax time — monitoring tax liability as it accumulates, identifying harvesting opportunities, and making informed decisions about when to take gains or losses. <a href="#coinLedger">Multi-year tax history</a> covers cryptocurrency activity going back multiple years for amended returns, audit support, or historical basis reconstruction.

<a href="#coinLedger">Subscription pricing tiers</a> scale with transaction volume — different plan levels accommodate the reporting needs from occasional investors to high-volume traders. <a href="#coinLedger">CoinLedger customer support</a> provides guidance on handling complex cryptocurrency tax situations through documentation, help center, and direct support channels.
